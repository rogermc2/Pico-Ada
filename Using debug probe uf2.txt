
Step 1  Turn the Pico 2 into a debugging tool for debugging other Pico 2s.
Flash the Pico 2 with debugprobe
Hold BOOTSEL
Plug into your Mac
A USB drive appears
Drag:
debugprobe_on_pico2.uf2
The board reboots automatically
After this, the Pico 2 is no longer a “normal” Pico—it’s now a debugger.

Step 2
What it becomes on your Mac
It enumerates as a CMSIS-DAP USB device. Drivers on macOS are not needed.
